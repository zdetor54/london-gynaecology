About Me

I am a Consultant Gynaecologist and Specialist in diagnostic gynaecologic oncology procedures, providing comprehensive care from first diagnosis of gynaecological cancer, including ovarian, cervical and womb cancer.

I provide one-stop gynaecology service for benign and potential cancerous conditions. My practice offers same-day appointments for ultrasound as well as hysteroscopy and colposcopy.

My principle of practice is to provide expert advice and up-to-date care, and to be accessible, ensuring that my service matches the holistic needs of my patients.
