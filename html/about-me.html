About Me

Farshad Tahmasebi

Mr Farshad Tahmasebi has a wealth of experience and expertise in the field of Obstetrics and Gynaecology, particularly in gynaecology, gynaecology oncology, colposcopy, vulvoscopy and laparoscopy. His involvement in creating integrated care pathways and quality improvement projects, as well as his published research, highlights his commitment to improving patient care and outcomes.

Mr Tahmasebi's experience covers general and acute gynaecology, rapid access, colposcopy, vulvoscopy, and gynaecology oncology sessions. He is responsible for ensuring cancer targets are met through the 2WW, demonstrating his ability to manage a diverse range of patients and situations.

His commitment to professional development and excellence, as well as his ability to inspire trust in patients and colleagues and lead medical teams to successful outcomes, makes him a valuable asset to any medical team.
