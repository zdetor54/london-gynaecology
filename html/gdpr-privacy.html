GDPR/Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At londongynaecology.com, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our patients' personal data. This GDPR policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information, as well as your rights regarding this data.

2. Data Collection

We collect personal data from our patients to provide medical services effectively. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, email, phone number)
  • Medical history and health-related data
  • Payment information (if applicable)

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Your personal data will be processed for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage medical services
  • To communicate with you regarding your health and appointments
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements

4. Access to Information

Patients have the right to access their personal data. You can request access to your information by contacting us through the following methods:

  • Phone: (0203) 368 8379

Upon receiving your request, we will verify your identity and provide you with a copy of your data within one month, as required by GDPR.

5. Data Rectification

If you believe that any of your personal data is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request rectification. Please contact us using the methods above, and we will correct any inaccuracies as soon as possible.

6. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances. If you wish to exercise this right, please contact us. We will review your request and inform you of the outcome within one month.

7. Data Protection Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, and destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Access controls and user authentication
  • Regular security assessments

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or through our website.

We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your personal data is handled in compliance with GDPR. Thank you for trusting londongynaecology.com with your healthcare needs.
