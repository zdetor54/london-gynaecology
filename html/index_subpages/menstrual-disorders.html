Help with Menstrual Problems

Through physical examinations, blood tests, and imaging like ultrasounds, I can help diagnose the underlying cause of irregular periods, heavy bleeding, painful cramps, and other menstrual issues. Once we have determined the severity and cause of the condition, we can recommend treatment options, including hormonal birth control, medication for pain management, lifestyle changes, or surgical interventions in some cases.

You will have the opportunity to share your thoughts, concerns, as well as discuss your diagnosis and treatment options. In many cases, this can be accomplished within a much shorter timeframe, sometimes on the same day. We will find the best treatment options which suits you and your lifestyle.
