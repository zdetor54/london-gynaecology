Ovarian Cysts

Diagnosed with Ovarian Cysts?

In order to diagnose and treat an ovarian cyst, I conduct pelvic exams and ultrasound exams, monitor its progress, and, if necessary, remove it surgically through a minimally invasive procedure called laparoscopy, especially if it is large, causing symptoms, or suspected to be cancerous. For prevention of future cysts, hormonal therapy may also be recommended.
